Mango Shake

Description

very yummy Mango Shake mmmmmmmmmm 😋😋😋

Ingredients

  • Mangoes
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Peel mangoes and slice edible flesh from pit. Cut flesh into chunks.
  2. In a blender. combine mangoes, milk, cold water, and ice. Process until smooth.
  3. To serve, pour into serving glasses and garnish with diced fresh mangoes if desired.
