Filipino Style Spaghetti (HALAL)

Description

Filipino spaghetti is a uniquely Filipino adaptation of traditional Italian spaghetti. Filipinos’ love for bold, sweet flavors, as seen in dishes like beef pares and tocino, led them to create a favorite pasta dish with a distinct sweetness. Banana ketchup and sugar, both staples in Filipino cooking, make up the base of the Filipino spaghetti sauce. It has now become an iconic dish that Filipinos love to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Pieces hotdogs or beef franks
  • Filipino style spaghetti sauce
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Beef broth
  • Medium Onion
  • Minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Cook the Spaghetti noodles according to package instructions. Once cooked, transfer to a bowl. Set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a Pan.
  3. Saute the onion and garlic.
  4. Once the onions becomes soft, add the ground beef. Cook until the color turns light brown.
  5. Add the hotdog. Stir and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Pour-in the Spaghetti sauce and beef broth. Stir and let boil. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
  7. Try to taste the sauce and add salt and pepper if needed.
  8. Pour the Filipino Style Spaghetti sauce over the Spaghetti. Top with shredded cheese.
  9. Serve. Share and enjoy!
